The MIT Movie Corpus is a semantically tagged training and test corpus in BIO format. The eng corpus are simple queries, and the trivia10k13 corpus are more complex queries. They can be found here:

https://groups.csail.mit.edu/sls/downloads/movie/

Please use the datasets above to train an algorithm to detect Entities. You can use any package or library, but you are expected to have a reasonably good understanding of it. Please ensure your code is readable with plenty of comments.

The deliverables will be:

- Code of the project (preferably in Jupyter Notebook or Google Colab)
- Performance Metrics, overall and per class
- Presentation with outline and steps
- Potential improvements

Please share the deliverables as a .zip attachment.
